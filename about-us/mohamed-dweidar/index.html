About Us

Mohamed Dweidar

Principal | Member

Mohamed Dweidar is a seasoned entrepreneur whose extensive experience across various industries fortifies his capacity to help other business owners create and scale profitable businesses. With a keen business acumen and a specialty in wealth and tax strategy, he leads a global team of CPAs and Certified Accountants.

As a business structure architect and wealth educator, he has held several executive positions including Founder and CEO of Accountants on Air, CEO of Vetmore Organization and President of Dweidson Holdings, Inc. Dweidar is also a CFO at MediaGO, Managing Partner Legacy TCP LLC and actively contributes to a GSA non-profit organization.

Additionally, he shares his expertise through mentorship at CUNY Baruch College's Zicklin Business School, where he earned his BBA in Accounting.
